Noor Mohammad Siam

Entrepreneur | Tech Enthusiast | Community Builder | Digital Contributor | IT Innovator

Assalamu Alaikum

Noor Mohammad Siam (Bengali: নুর মোহাম্মদ সিয়াম) , known online as N M Siam — a technology enthusiast and aspiring IT leader from the historic district of Cumilla. He is a Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) student at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). Alongside his academic journey, he serves as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Managing Director of Mozammal Hoque Market (A Concern of Mozammal Hoque Group), where he lead operations and strategic development. Beyond entrepreneurship, he actively contribute to Bangla Wikipedia to expand open access to knowledge and engage as a Google Maps Local Guide to strengthen digital connectivity. Siam’s professional interests lie in software development, digital strategy, and IT entrepreneurship, and his mission is to merge innovation, leadership, and community-driven technology to create meaningful impact in the digital era.
